"Temp YT Playlist" is an interactive web application that lets users create temporary, personalized music playlists with ease. With a simple, user-friendly interface, users can add videos to their playlists and enjoy a clean, ambient viewing mode with autoplay, perfect for uninterrupted listening. Whether you’re a music lover or presenting videos, Temp YT Playlist makes it easy to explore new sounds without the hassle of managing permanent playlists.
